Our Team

Julie Brennan

Branding / Identity: Cross-Media Marketing

Born and raised in Vieques, Puerto Rico, Julie has over 25 years of experience in the fields of marketing and advertising. Her interest in writing began when she worked as the Public Affairs Specialist for the Morale, Welfare and Recreation Department at the US Naval Base is Puerto Rico. Creating interested in companies, services and people, she has also written for various publications over the years.

Through the years she has met a wide range of outstanding individuals whose expertise she now shares with others as part of MarketComplete. She has assembled the MC team to provide creative strategies tailored to each clients’ specific needs and goals.

Julie is the publisher of My Forsyth magazine.

Rachel Buckley

Editor

Rachel Buckley is a freelance writer with experience in editing and fact checking. She has covered a variety of topics such as travel, motor sports, fine dining, restaurant, independent film, live theater reviews and fashion for lifestyle magazines.

Rachel specializes in creative writing. She is a published poet and an award winning playwright. Rachel is the Editor of My Forsyth magazine. She may be reached at Rachel@myforsyth.com.

NLB Studios

Print & Graphic/Web Design

NLB Studios is committed to: creating an environment of integrity and accountability; training in print, design, and interpersonal skills for men in addiction recovery; serving each client with the highest respect and integrity; providing the highest quality design, print, and media solutions; and advancing No Longer Bound’s mission of rescuing addicts, reconciling families, and regenerating men.

Kim Bates

Kim Bates Photography

Kim Bates is an award winning professional freelance photographer. He is the current President and Co-Founder of the Cherokee Photography Club, President of the North Georgia Camera Club Council and serves as Secretary on the Executive Board of the Cherokee Arts Center. His photographic specialties includes weddings and events, portraits and fine arts.

Kim is the photographer for various publications, including My Forsyth Magazine and Around About Local Media. He has also photographed for the United Way for Forsyth County, Studio 101, Downtown Woodstock Development Authority, Northeast and Northwest Atlanta Vascular Care Centers as well as numerous portraits of sports and political figures in Metro Atlanta.

www.kimbatesphotoart.com
